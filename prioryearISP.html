Concept

The concept of my ISP from last year was a platformer style game. A platformer game is a 2D game where a character has to move around and jump from platform to platfrom to get to the finish and complete the level.

Motivation

The motovation for making a platformer game was looking back on what games I had fun playing when I was younger and immediately Mario came to mind. Making a game similar to Mario seemed like it would be a fun project as well as being simple enough to finish in the time given.

Conclusions

When coding this ISP almost nothing went as expected. There would always be some kind of error with the game being proportional to the canvas size of the hitboxes of the charater, platforms, and lava not interacting as they should. In the end we were able to fix the issues one at a time and the project turned out pretty good and it was working well by the time presentation day came around.
