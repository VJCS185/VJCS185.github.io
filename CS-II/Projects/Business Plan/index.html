Child Detection in Unattended Cars


Identity

   Saving the lives of many young children and babies using AI Image detection to detect if the children are left alone in hot cars.


Problem

    Many adults in the United States with young children or babies and have a misconception of being able to leave children in a car for up to half and hour. There are also many accidents in which children are left alone in cars, for example both parents could think that the other parent is taking the baby out of the car and end up leaving the baby in the garage in the hot car leading to the child or baby's unfortunate death. In the United States alone nearly 40 children die in hot cars every year.


Solution

   With the use of temperature sensors and cameras installed inside cars, when temeratures over 95 degrees fahrenheit or 35 degrees celsius are detected inside a car, a camera will scan for a baby or young child using AI image recognition. It will then nofity the driver through their cellphone with an application. If the problem is not resolved within 3 minutes, the police will be notified.


Target Market

   Their are two target markets. The first target market is car companies which would install the system as a part of constructing the vehicle. The second target market is parents. It would be sold as a kit to install into a car in stores around the United States and later globally.


Competition

   Currenly there are companines working on products to solve this issue, although there are not any high quility products with the same objective being sold currently.


Revenue Streams

   There are 2 revenue steams for this product which are online selling through our website or 3rd party websites such as Amazon. The other revenue stream is selling to stores around the country and later globally.


Marketing Activites

   The product will be adversited through websites that have high online traffic, televesion, popular social media, and advertised to car companies directly.


Expenses

   Expenses for the business will be in the research and development of the product. Once R&D is completed expenses will include the cost for the harwarde to create the product, inventory space, and the cost of advertisement.

Milestones

   Milestones for this project include completeing research and development with a working prototype, advertising the product to the target markets, and selling the product to car companies and stores around the United States and later globally.
